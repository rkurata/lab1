Caramel Banana Bread

(originally found here: www.tasteandtellblog.com)

Ingredients

    Banana Bread:

  • 1 3/4 c flour
  • 2/3 c brown sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 c mashed bananas
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 2 eggs

Caramel Glaze:

  • 1/4 c butter
  • 1/2 c brown sugar
  • 2 tsp heavy cream
  • 1 c powdered sugar

    		•

    Steps

      Banana Bread

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Prepare a loaf pan with non-stick spray.
    2. Mix the flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together. Add in the bananas and milk and mix for two minutes. Then add in the eggs.
    3. Pour the mixture into the sprayed pan and bake for about one hour, or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted.
    4. Cool completely, then remove from the loaf pan and top with caramel glaze.

      5. Caramel Glaze:

    5. Combine the butter and brown sugar in a saucepan. Bring it to a boil, then remove from the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes.
    6. Whisk in the cream and powdered sugar until all lumps disappear. Pour over the cooled banana bread.
